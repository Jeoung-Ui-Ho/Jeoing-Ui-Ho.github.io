    Recipes


    Here're my strongly recommend recipes. I swear worth to try.

    1. Basic highball

      2. 1 shot of Any Whisky + 3 shot of sparkling water

      Incresing flavor of whisky and lower proof to reduce hangover.

    2. Midori sour

      3. 1 shot of Midori + 3 Shot of sprite + 1 shot of blue lemonade

      Original recipe use sweet&sour mix, but during my experience blue lemonade make taste more sweet and smooth.
      And blue color helps green color more vivid. it looks good!

    3. Party drink

      4. 1 shot of vodka, white rum, triple sec + 9 shot of juice(good for grape or orange)

      It's my original recipe. very sweet and amplify the taste of juice.
      Going in smooth, but it has high proof. Take care of drinking
